LANGUAGES

The official languages of the state are Nepali, Sikkimese, Lepcha and English.
Additional official languages include Gurung, Limbu, Magar, Mukhia, Newar,
Rai, Sherpa and Tamang for the purpose of preservation of culture and tradition in the state.

Nepali is the lingua franca of Sikkim, while Sikkimese (Bhutia) and Lepcha are spoken in certain areas.
English is also spoken and understood in most of Sikkim.
Other languages include Dzongkha, Groma, Hindi, Majhi, Majhwar, Thulung, Tibetan, and Yakha.
